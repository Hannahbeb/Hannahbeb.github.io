Mobile App to Improve Job Performance

The goal of this collaborative UX final project was to prototype a mobile application in Figma that could improve job performance. This project involved empathy research, journey mapping, ideation, prototyping, and usability testing.

Empathy Research

After identifying our target user, we created empathy diagrams by assuming what our users thought, said, felt, and did in their job. Then in order to gain accurate insight into our target user, we conducted empathy interviews by first preparing interview questions and reaching out to potential interviewees. The result of this stage was an empathy video that recapped the interview.

Journey Mapping

In order to understand our users' pains and gains, we built a journey map that visually displays the various tasks our users complete at their daily job. This stage informed our prototype and helped us truly understand our user.

Ideation

After analyzing our users' pains, we began ideating different features that could help our users accomplish their tasks quicker and more efficiently. We brainstormed as a group using the Figjam Figma feature. Once our ideation was complete, we chose the best 4 features to begin prototyping.

Prototyping

Once our goal oriented tasks and features were identified, we began protoyping a mobile app solution in Figma. We focused on organization and flow in our prototype. Feel free to check out the interactive Figma prototype below.

Usability Testing

When the prototype was complete, we launched into user testing where we learned how to create a task list and write a script for the interviews. Each of us recorded data on the users as they were completing the tasks. This step was incredibly insightful and helped us realize what adjustments we would make in the future.

We got great feedback from our users during the tests on why they were so confused with the calendar portion of the app. The hamburger menu/ pop out screen was confusing people. Looking back, we realized that it would be more user friendly if we scratched the pop out menu and just put the different calendar option checkboxes at the top, directly on the main screen.

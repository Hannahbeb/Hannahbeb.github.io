BGSU Honors Project

As my final project for the Bowling Green State University Honor's College, I had the opportunity to research the environmental impacts of web development and put digital sustainability best practices to the test.

Research Question

How can front-end web developers build websites that are energy efficient and

Methodology

Research

Understand the environmental impacts of the digital world and find resources on sustainable web development through an investigative deep dive.

Apply

Build 2 versions of a website for a practice client. Version 1 will be built normally with no regard to digital sustainability. Version 2 will apply the best practices gathered from phase 1. The 2 versions of the site will be compared.

Evaluate

Determine the environmental impacts of both versions of the site and if the sustainable practices improve site energy efficiency.

Conclusions

Applying sustainable web development best practices improved site energy efficiency and reduced estimated carbon output.

Applying the sustainable best practices did not add an unreasonable amount of development time.

Sustainable web development could have incredible environmental impacts if the knowledge and best practices become more well known.
